ACM.Industry

Working as a part of UCSB's ACM chapter, I cofounded the local business tech consulting branch: ACM.Industry

The branch builds static websites, apps, webapps, and more for local Santa Barbara businesses which lacked the resources to develop these on their own.

The branch takes on projects which it then delegates to its members, helping to expose students to industry-level projects and project management techniques.
